PROJECT BRIEF: AUTO FREE
An Open-Source, Frictionless Infrastructure Gift to Humanity for Planetary Repair and Space Exploration
The Vision: To completely sever global transport from fossil fuels, eliminate the mechanical wear and tear that destroys our roads, and use the daily movement of humanity to fund our future in the stars. No one owns it. No one is in charge. It belongs to the planet.

1. The Core Infrastructure: How It Works
Instead of tearing up existing tarmac, causing decades of traffic delays and expensive roadworks, Auto Free utilizes space that already runs continuously along our motorways and highways: the drainage gullies.

The Track: Pre-fabricated, waterproof, modular electromagnetic rails are dropped directly into existing drainage systems. The bottom half of the module is hollow, allowing floodwater to flow completely unobstructed, while the top half houses sealed, high-voltage coils.

The Vehicles: Cars and lorries are stripped of heavy diesel engines, gearboxes, and massive, toxic multi-ton battery packs. They become lightweight "digital shells" equipped with a computerized magnetic belly-plate and a retractable arm. The arm hovers 5cm over the drainage gully, locking onto the track.

The Propulsion: The road uses synchronous linear induction to generate moving magnetic waves. The vehicle handles no weight and experiences zero tire friction, floating smoothly at 70 mph.

The Power Nodes: Motorway service stations are converted into localized battery power vaults. They absorb cheap, excess renewable energy (like wind and solar) from the national grid at night and pump it directly into the road gullies during peak daytime traffic.

The Hybrid Junction System: Vehicles keep standard rubber tires and a tiny "pocket battery." When crossing a junction where the magnet gaps out, the car's brains smoothly transfer weight to the wheels for a few seconds using standard electric power, before automatically locking back onto the gully on the other side.

2. The Global Revenue Loop: Show Me the Mula
Auto Free completely eliminates fuel costs and slashes vehicle manufacturing prices in half. In return, users pay a Dynamic, Pay-As-You-Flow Infrastructure Tariff automatically tracked per mile by the vehicle's computer brains.

Because vehicles are cheap to run and require virtually zero mechanical maintenance (no tire blowouts, no air brakes to replace), individuals and logistics firms save thousands of pounds a year while generating massive, self-sustaining revenue for the planet.

The Proposed Global Tariffs:
Tier 1 (Commuter Cars): 5p per mile

Tier 2 (Delivery Vans): 12p per mile

Tier 3 (Rigid Medium Trucks): 25p per mile

Tier 4 (Articulated 44-Ton Lorries): 60p per mile

Using a high-traffic corridor like the UK's M25 motorway as a baseline, a 20-mile average commute across 200,000 daily vehicles generates over £460,000 per day (£168 Million a year) from just one single highway loop.

3. The Ultimate Gift: Where the Money Goes
This project is a decentralized legacy project for the Earth. To prevent corruption, corporate greed, or political interference, the revenue is automatically split and digitally hard-coded to stream directly into three independent accounts:

The Regional Upkeep Account (approx. 20%): Sent directly to the service station power nodes to maintain the physical gully coils and purchase wholesale green electricity. The system is entirely self-funding and requires zero taxpayer money.

The Global Space Exploration Fund (approx. 40%): Handed directly to international, collaborative space bodies (such as UNOOSA and global space agencies). This creates a permanent, infinite funding engine to build permanent lunar bases, explore Mars, and push humanity into the galaxy, funded entirely by the traffic of Earth.

The Planetary Repair Initiative (approx. 40%): Managed by worldwide climate scientists and ecologist unions. This fund is strictly locked to pay for massive planetary healing projects: cleaning plastics from the oceans, massive reforestation, and building global carbon-capture networks.

There is no Prime Minister, no President, and no corporate CEO in charge of Auto Free. All blueprint designs, software code, and safety protocols are completely open-source.

We invite a global, peer-reviewed dream team of experts to openly collaborate, stress-test, and build this framework for the benefit of all mankind:
